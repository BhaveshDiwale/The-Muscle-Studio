About Us

We would love to introduce ourselves

The Muscle Studio was founded in 2012 with an aim to develop a regular habit of workout among individuals. The founder of the organization, Mr. R.K. Agastya had in mind the difficulties faced by people in their later life due to no workouts in the prime age.

Vision

Our vision is to help each and every individual attain their best physique and also work on internal strengthening through yoga.

Each new day is an opportunity to improve yourself. Take it and make the most of it.
